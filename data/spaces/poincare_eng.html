Poincaré sphere

Description
A manifold which is not homeomorphic to a sphere but has the same homology group as a sphere. This is derived by suitable identifications of facets of a regular dodecahedron.
(Identify the vertices with the same label in the figure below.)
The data given here is a triangulation made by Björner and Lutz. This is the smallest triangulation currently known. (16 vertices and 90 facets)
Some property
This is Cohen-Macaulay, but not constructible nor shellable.
