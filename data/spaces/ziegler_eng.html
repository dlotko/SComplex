Ziegler's 3-ball

Description
Ziegler's 1998 paper showed a very small non-shellable 3-ball which is even smaller thatn Gru"nbaum's ball. This amazing example has only 10 vertices and 21 facets. (Believed to be the smallest.)
Properties
This is known to be constructible.
