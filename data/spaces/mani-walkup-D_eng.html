Mani & Walkup's 3-sphere(D)

Description
Mani and Walkup's 3-sphere which is not vertex decomposable. This example is made from their another sphere (mani-walkup-C.dat) to reduce the number of faces a little. Called "D" in the paper.
Properties
In the paper, it is written that whether this 3-sphere is shellable or not known to the authors. But my recent calculation on the computer showed that this sphere is shellable. Here is a shelling.
