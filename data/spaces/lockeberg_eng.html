Lockeberg's 4-polytope

Description
This 4-dimensional polytope was shown in Lockeberg's PhD thesis in 1977 and this is an example whose boundary complex is not vertex decomposable. Reviewd in the article by Klee & Kleinschmidt.
Properties
This is shellable because polytopes are shellable.
