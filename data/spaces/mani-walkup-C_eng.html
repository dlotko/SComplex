Mani & Walkup's 3-sphere(C)

Description
A 3-sphere made by Mani and Walkup, which is not vertex decomposable This is made in relation to the Hirsh conjecture, that the w_v path conjecture fails for general (non-polytopal) spheres. Called "C" in the paper.
Properties
This sphere is shellable.
